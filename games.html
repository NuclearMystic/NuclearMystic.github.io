Altius

Altius is a first person 3D, casual, underwater exploration game similar to games like Abzu or Treasures of the Deep. The objective is to find the different items scattered across the map, and explore. There are 25 chests, 6 gems, and many coins. Altius was made for the Brackey's game jam 2023.2, and finished in 173rd place out of over 900 entries.

A few highlights from the project include:

  • -Controller created from scratch, using thruster based controls.
  • -Queue system for hints found in chests, so no matter what order you open the chests, the notes display in the sequence intended.
  • -Used post processing effects to create underwater view effect.

Return to Castle Frankenstein

In Return to Castle Frankenstein, you step into the shoes of Doctor Frankenstein's estranged child, who has just inherited the enigmatic Castle. Invited to explore the eerie depths of this ancestral abode, you find yourself face to face with Frankenstein's legendary monster. Can you overcome the legacy of your father and the monstrosity he created, or will you succumb to the darkness that looms within the castle's walls?

Return to Castle Frankenstein was created for the Scream Jam 2023, and ranked 35 out of almost 500 entries.

A few highlights from the project include:

  • -Controller coded with Unity's character controller component.
  • -Note system with hints scattered around the castle.
  • -Used post processing effects to create black and white aesthetics.
  • -Coded switches and levers to activate different effects around the lab to give it that mad scientist feel.
  • -Created monster AI from scratch.

Dino Door Dash

Dino Door Dash is a 2D, side scrolling, adventure game where you play as a little green dinosaur who gets lost in time and space, and must venture through strange doors that lead to surreal worlds with various challenges. This game was my first time working in 2D, and I ended up having a lot of fun with it.

This game was created in a week for the Brackey's game jam 2024.1.

A few highlights from the project include:

  • -2D controller using rigid body physics for movement.
  • -Animated 2D sprites.
  • -HP system with enemy drops that heal the player.
  • -Multi character dialogue system coded from scratch.
  • -Coded 2D enemy AI.
  • -Used a web GL build to allow the game to be played in a browser.
